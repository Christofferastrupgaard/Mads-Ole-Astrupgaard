About Mads-Ole

  • Took over management of a Danish company and organization and after a needed turnaround 15mEUR in revenue and negative EBT grew to 100mEUR in revenue and a solid EBT.
  • Built up an international company with locations on different continents and global supplier and customer base.
  • Established sales offices in US and China and assembly facility in China.
  • Strong knowledge about the green transition and electrification.
  • Well-connected in the global supply chain in the wind industry.
  • Personal relations at C-level with OEMs and developers in wind internationally.
  • Completed several M&As to grow the business and consolidate different business areas.
  • Divesting business areas and companies to consolidate for the good of the company and the shareholder.
  • Extensive experience with hybrid management as a part of the leadership required when having companies in different countries and promoting global cooperation.

  • Over the course of 18 years, I have worked with the global wind industry focusing on the entire value chain: From subcontractors until the wind turbine is erected and connected to the grid. Via close collaborations with global OEM customers, international suppliers, and colleagues and cooperation partners in the industry, the range of tasks have included idea generation and development of customer-specific solutions and systems, prototypes, 0-series, series production and marketing. As responsible for the entire value chain, I have developed close relations throughout the industry.
  • In addition, I have been dedicated to the wind industry in general as member of the board of Danish Wind Industry Association/Wind Denmark (today Green Power Denmark) for 15 years - 7 years as chairman of the board. The wind industry today stands as a very strong and central player of the existing and very much the future energy system, especially in Denmark and in the EU, but also globally.
